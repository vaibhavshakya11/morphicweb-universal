About

MorphicWeb Universal — a privacy-first accessibility suite that adapts any website to your needs, on your device.

  • Private by default. Everything runs locally; cloud AI is opt-in with your own key.
  • Language Insights (readability, parts of speech, entities, passive voice) need no download.
  • Shortcuts: Alt+Shift+M on/off · Alt+Shift+R read aloud · Alt+Shift+O show original.
  • Every change is reversible with Show original.
